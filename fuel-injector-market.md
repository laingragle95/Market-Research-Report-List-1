<p><ul><li>Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI)</li><li>Port Fuel Injectors (PFI)</li><li>Diesel Fuel Injectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel injector market encompasses several types, including Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI), Port Fuel Injectors (PFI), and Diesel Fuel Injectors. GDI systems inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing fuel efficiency and power. PFI systems, on the other hand, inject fuel into the intake manifold, promoting better mixing with air and smoother operation. Diesel fuel injectors operate at higher pressures, atomizing fuel for efficient combustion, crucial for diesel engines. Each type is tailored for specific engine performance and emission standards.</p></p>

<p><p>The Whole Body Cryotherapy market comprises three main types: Liquid Nitrogen Therapy, Dry Ice Therapy, and Electric Therapy. Liquid Nitrogen Therapy utilizes nitrogen to rapidly cool the body, offering deep tissue benefits. Dry Ice Therapy employs solid carbon dioxide for localized cooling, impacting surface areas effectively. Electric Therapy uses electrically cooled chambers, controlling temperature and minimizing risks associated with extreme cold. Each method caters to distinct user preferences and regulatory considerations, making them popular choices in wellness and recovery spaces.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Nebuliser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebuliser</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebuliser</li><li>Mesh Nebuliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nebuliser market comprises three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulisers. Pneumatic nebulisers use compressed air to aerosolize medication, making them widely available and cost-effective. Ultrasonic nebulisers utilize high-frequency sound waves to create mist, offering faster medication delivery and quieter operation, ideal for home use. Mesh nebulisers employ a vibrating mesh or membrane to generate aerosol, providing portability and efficient drug delivery, often favored for patient comfort and ease of use. Each type serves distinct therapeutic needs.</p></p>

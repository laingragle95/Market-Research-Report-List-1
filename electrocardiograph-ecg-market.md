<p><p>The Electrocardiograph (ECG) market is segmented into two main types: monitoring ECG systems and diagnostic ECG systems. Monitoring ECG systems are designed for continuous observation of heart activity, typically used in hospitals and critical care settings to track patients' heart rhythms in real time. In contrast, diagnostic ECG systems are utilized for identifying and analyzing heart conditions, providing detailed assessments through techniques like stress testing and Holter monitoring. Both types are essential for effective cardiovascular care and management.</p></p>

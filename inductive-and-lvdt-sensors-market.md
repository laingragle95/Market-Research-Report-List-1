<p><p>The inductive and LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) sensors market is categorized into two main types based on their operational power: DC operated LVDT sensors and AC operated LVDT sensors. DC operated LVDT sensors utilize direct current for their functionality, making them suitable for applications requiring low power consumption and stable measurements. In contrast, AC operated LVDT sensors use alternating current, offering advantages in noise immunity and better accuracy in dynamic measurements, expanding their usage in industrial and automation applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Transcranial Doppler devices are utilized for assessing cerebral blood flow and diagnosing conditions like stroke. The market includes M-mode display and B-mode display types. M-mode display provides a single-dimensional view of blood flow dynamics over time, allowing for precise measurement of velocities, while B-mode display offers a two-dimensional imaging of blood vessels, aiding in visualizing flow patterns and abnormalities. These distinctions enhance diagnostic capabilities, catering to different clinical needs in neurology and neurosurgery settings.</p></p>

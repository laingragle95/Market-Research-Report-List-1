<p><p>The Automatic Screen Changers market features two primary types: Single Piston and Double Piston systems. Single Piston changers use one piston to control the screen replacement process, offering a simpler design and usually lower cost, ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, Double Piston changers utilize two pistons, allowing for continuous operation and minimal downtime, making them suitable for high-production settings. Each type caters to different industry needs, balancing efficiency, cost, and throughput requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>The Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) is a power semiconductor device that combines the advantages of both MOSFETs and bipolar transistors, enabling efficient switching and amplification of electrical power. The IGBT market is segmented into two primary types: IGBT modules and discrete IGBTs. IGBT modules consist of multiple IGBT chips packaged together for high-power applications, offering improved thermal performance and reduced assembly complexity. Discrete IGBTs, on the other hand, are single units suited for lower-power applications, providing flexibility in design and easier integration.</p></p>

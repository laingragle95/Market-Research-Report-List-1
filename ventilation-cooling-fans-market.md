<p><p>The ventilation cooling fans market comprises various types, primarily Axial Fans, Centrifugal Fans, and Others. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan shaft, ideal for cooling and ventilation in residential and industrial settings due to their efficiency and low noise. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, create airflow at right angles to the intake, offering high pressure and airflow, making them suitable for HVAC systems and large spaces. 'Others' includes specialized designs like propeller and mixed-flow fans, catering to unique applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The boom lift tire market typically includes three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering excellent shock absorption and traction on uneven terrains, making them suitable for outdoor use. Solid tires, made from rubber, provide durability and resistance to punctures but offer a harsher ride on rough surfaces. Polyurethane tires combine lightweight properties with minimal wear, making them ideal for indoor applications where floor protection and low maintenance are essential. Each type serves specific operational needs.</p></p>

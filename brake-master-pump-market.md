<p><ul><li>Vacuum Pump</li><li>Hydraulic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Brake Master Pump market is primarily divided into two types: vacuum pumps and hydraulic pumps. Vacuum pumps assist in creating low pressure to help enhance brake response by reducing air resistance, leading to efficient braking. Conversely, hydraulic pumps utilize fluid pressure to facilitate the braking process, ensuring smooth and reliable operation. Both types are integral to modern braking systems, with vacuum pumps generally used in lighter vehicles and hydraulic pumps favored in heavier vehicles for their robust performance.</p></p>

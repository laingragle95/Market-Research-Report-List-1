Gear pumps utilize rotating gears to move fluid, offering consistent flow rates and high efficiency, suitable for industrial uses. Other pumps may include diaphragm and peristaltic pumps, catering to specialized applications or unique fluid characteristics, thereby expanding the versatility of dispensing solutions.</p></p>

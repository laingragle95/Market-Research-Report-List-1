<p><p>The Quad Flat No-leads (QFN) package market consists primarily of two types: air-cavity QFN and plastic-moulded QFN. Air-cavity QFN packages feature an encapsulated cavity, enhancing thermal performance and reducing weight, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. In contrast, plastic-moulded QFNs are fully encapsulated in plastic, providing robust protection and cost-effectiveness, ideal for mass-produced consumer electronics. Both types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and IoT, driven by the demand for compact, efficient packaging solutions.</p></p>

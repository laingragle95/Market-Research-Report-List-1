<p><p>The E-paper Display (EPD) chip market is segmented into two main types: Discrete Controllers and System on Chip (SoC). Discrete Controllers are specialized chips that manage specific tasks, allowing for flexibility in design but requiring external components for complete systems. In contrast, SoC integrates all necessary functions onto a single chip, offering compactness and energy efficiency. This integration simplifies design processes, reduces overall system size, and is increasingly favored for modern applications in e-readers, smart labels, and digital signage.</p></p>

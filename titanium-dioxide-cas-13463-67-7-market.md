<p><p>Titanium Dioxide (TiO2), classified under CAS 13463-67-7, is a vital white pigment used in various applications, including paints, coatings, and plastics. The market for TiO2 is segmented into two primary production processes: the sulfate process and the chloride process. The sulfate process involves treating titanium ore with sulfuric acid, resulting in a lower-cost but less pure product. In contrast, the chloride process utilizes chlorine and is known for producing high-quality TiO2, preferred for premium applications. Both processes significantly influence market dynamics and pricing.</p></p>

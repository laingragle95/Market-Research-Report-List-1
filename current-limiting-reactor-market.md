<p><strong>The Current Limiting Reactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Reactor</li><li>Series Reactor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current limiting reactor market is segmented into shunt reactors and series reactors. Shunt reactors are used to manage voltage levels and reactive power compensation, particularly in high voltage transmission systems, while absorbing excess reactive power to stabilize the grid. Series reactors, on the other hand, are connected in line with the circuit and primarily serve to limit fault currents, enhancing system protection and reliability. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring efficient and stable electrical networks.</p></p>

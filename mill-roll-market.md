<p><p>Mill roll market types are categorized into hot rolling mill rolls and cold rolling mill rolls, each serving distinct applications in metal processing. Hot rolling mill rolls are used for shaping and forming materials at high temperatures, enhancing ductility and reducing hardness. In contrast, cold rolling mill rolls operate at room temperature, refining the material's surface finish and dimensional accuracy. Both types are critical in industries such as automotive, construction, and manufacturing, contributing to the efficiency and quality of metal products.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Articulated Robots</li><li>Parallel Robots</li><li>SCARA Robots</li><li>Cylindrical Robots</li><li>Cartesian Robots</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial robots market features several types, each serving different applications. Articulated robots are versatile and widely used for tasks requiring flexibility, such as welding and assembly. Parallel robots offer high precision for assembly and packaging, thanks to their symmetrical structure. SCARA robots excel in horizontal movement, ideal for pick-and-place tasks. Cylindrical robots combine rotational and linear motion for tasks like assembly and material handling. Cartesian robots, or linear robots, utilize a three-axis system for precise positioning in applications such as machining and 3D printing.</p></p>

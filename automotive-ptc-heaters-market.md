<p><p>The automotive PTC heaters market includes two primary types: PTC water heaters and PTC air heaters. PTC water heaters utilize positive temperature coefficient technology to efficiently heat coolant for engine warmth, enhancing performance during cold starts. In contrast, PTC air heaters are designed to warm the cabin air, ensuring passenger comfort. Both systems leverage the self-regulating properties of PTC materials, offering energy efficiency and safety by preventing overheating, making them essential for modern vehicle heating solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>The Automatic Testing Systems market can be categorized into two main types: Wafer Automatic Testing Systems and Device Automatic Testing Systems. Wafer Automatic Testing Systems focus on testing semiconductor wafers during the fabrication process, ensuring quality and functionality before individual chips are cut and packaged. In contrast, Device Automatic Testing Systems are designed for testing finished semiconductor devices, such as integrated circuits and chips, evaluating performance and reliability before they are deployed in various applications. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in manufacturing.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Blade Type Steering Pump</li><li>Gear Type Steering Pump</li><li>Plunger Type Steering Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steering pump market consists of three main types: blade type, gear type, and plunger type steering pumps. Blade type steering pumps use blades to create pressure, providing smooth steering response. Gear type steering pumps utilize meshing gears to generate hydraulic pressure, known for their reliability and efficiency. Plunger type steering pumps operate with a plunger mechanism, offering high pressure and compact design, suitable for performance vehicles. Each type caters to specific automotive needs, enhancing vehicle handling and control.</p></p>

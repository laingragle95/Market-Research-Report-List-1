<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Sensor Heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic</li><li>Extrinsic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic sensor heads can be categorized into two main types: intrinsic and extrinsic. Intrinsic sensors utilize the fiber itself as both the sensing element and the transmission medium, allowing for high sensitivity and minimal interference. In contrast, extrinsic sensors employ external components, such as mirrors and lenses, to measure changes in the light signal caused by environmental factors. Both types play crucial roles in various applications, including structural health monitoring, temperature sensing, and pressure measurements, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

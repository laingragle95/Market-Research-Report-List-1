<p><ul><li>Variable</li><li>Fixed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microwave attenuator market comprises two main types: variable and fixed. Variable attenuators allow for adjustable signal attenuation, enabling precise control over signal strength, often used in testing and calibration applications. Fixed attenuators provide a predetermined level of signal reduction, offering stable performance in applications where consistent attenuation is required. Both types serve critical roles in telecommunications, broadcasting, and medical devices, ensuring optimal signal quality and preventing distortion in various microwave frequency applications.</p></p>

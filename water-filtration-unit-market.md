<p><p>The water-filtration unit market comprises various types designed for specific purification needs. Bag and cartridge filters trap sediments and contaminants efficiently. Sediment filters focus on reducing sand, dirt, and rust, maintaining water clarity. Reverse osmosis filters ensure thorough purification by removing dissolved solids and microorganisms, catering to both residential and commercial applications for safe drinking water.</p></p>

<p><p>The point-to-point antenna market is segmented into three main types: Parabolic, Flat Panel, and Yagi antennas. Parabolic antennas offer high gain and directivity, making them ideal for long-distance communication. Flat panel antennas are compact and lightweight, suitable for areas with limited space while providing good performance. Yagi antennas are known for their directional properties and are commonly used in applications like television reception and amateur radio. Each type addresses specific needs in communication and broadcasting sectors.</p></p>

<p><p>The nail polish market comprises primarily two types: organic solvent-based and water-based formulations. Organic solvent-based nail polishes use volatile solvents for quicker drying and a glossy finish, making them popular for long-lasting wear. However, they may contain chemicals that can be harsh on nails. In contrast, water-based nail polishes are formulated with water as the primary solvent, making them less toxic and more environmentally friendly, but they typically offer shorter wear time and require careful application to avoid chipping.</p></p>

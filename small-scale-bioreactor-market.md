<p><p>The small-scale bioreactor market includes two main types: reusable bioreactors and single-use bioreactors. Reusable bioreactors are designed for multiple cycles of production, offering durability and cost-effectiveness for long-term operations in laboratory and industrial settings. In contrast, single-use bioreactors are pre-sterilized and intended for one-time use, minimizing contamination risks and reducing the need for cleaning validation. Both options cater to diverse applications in biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and research, with varying advantages based on operational needs and production scale.</p></p>

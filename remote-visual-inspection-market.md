<p><ul><li>Comparison Measurement</li><li>Shadow Measurement</li><li>Stereo Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Remote Visual Inspection (RVI) market comprises three primary measurement types: Comparison Measurement, Shadow Measurement, and Stereo Measurement. Comparison Measurement involves evaluating an object against a known reference for quality control. Shadow Measurement utilizes light and shadow effects to assess dimensions and defects, emphasizing precision. Stereo Measurement employs dual cameras to create 3D representations, enhancing depth perception and accuracy in inspection. Each method offers unique advantages, catering to various industrial needs, from manufacturing to aerospace, for efficient and thorough inspections.</p></p>

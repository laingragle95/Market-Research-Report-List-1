<p><p>The iToF (indirect Time of Flight) sensor market can be categorized into three main types: cwToF (Continuous Wave Time of Flight), which uses a continuous light source to measure distance; gToF (Geiger Mode Time of Flight), which employs photon counting for high-precision range measurements; and traditional iToF sensors, which utilize pulsed light for distance estimation. Each type offers distinct advantages, with applications ranging from automotive and robotics to consumer electronics and augmented reality, enhancing depth perception and enabling advanced imaging capabilities.</p></p>

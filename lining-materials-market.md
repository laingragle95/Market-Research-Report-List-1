<p><p>The lining materials market consists of three main categories: natural fibers, artificial fibers, and synthetic fibers. Artificial fibers, manufactured through chemical processes, mimic natural fibers and provide enhanced durability and versatility. Synthetic fibers, created from petrochemicals, are known for their strength, moisture resistance, and affordability, making them popular in various applications. Together, these materials meet diverse consumer demands across industries such as fashion, automotive, and home furnishings.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Ablative Laser</li><li>Nonablative Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser resurfacing machines market is divided into two main types: ablative and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layers of skin, promoting collagen production and significantly improving skin texture and wrinkles. These lasers typically lead to more pronounced results but involve longer recovery times. In contrast, non-ablative lasers target deeper skin layers without damaging the outer skin, offering subtle improvements with shorter downtime. Both types cater to different skin concerns and patient preferences in cosmetic and dermatological treatments.</p></p>

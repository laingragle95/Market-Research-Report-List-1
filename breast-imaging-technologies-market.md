<p><p>The breast imaging technologies market is divided into two main types: ionizing and non-ionizing breast imaging technologies. </p><p>Ionizing breast imaging technologies, such as mammography, utilize radiation to capture detailed images of breast tissue, aiding in cancer detection. In contrast, non-ionizing breast imaging technologies include modalities like ultrasound and MRI, which do not involve radiation and are often used for further evaluation or in specific patient scenarios. Both types play crucial roles in breast cancer screening and diagnosis.</p></p>
